Welcome to upStart

Welcome to our short-format courses where we’ve compressed our learning experience to fit your weekend. Try as many courses as you’d like, and take home a certification at the end of each course - it’s on us.

 

In this 100% self-paced course, you’ll be learning about problem-solving skills in Python using various elements such as Strings, Lists, and Dictionaries.

 

We’ve roped in Sajan Kedia, straight from Myntra, leading the Data Science Team of Pricing to show you the ropes. Over the next 15hrs, he will take you through a series of concepts interspersed with pop-up questions and short assignments. 

 

Since this is an advanced course, we recommend that you complete the ‘Introduction to Python’ course before starting this one. 

 

Who this course is for: Python developers, Product Managers, Data Analysts, Data Scientists, Machine Learning Engineers, Financial advisors, Software developers, Python developers. 

 

If you’re looking for deeper knowledge along with extensive career and mentorship support, you can join us for a Executive PG Programme in Data Science, a 12-month program with IIIT-B.

 

Housekeeping Details:

  • Use the discussion forum to ask any questions you may have about the content.

  • Keep applying the concepts covered in this course to your day-to-day professional life to sharpen these skills further.

 

With over 20 million hours of learning delivered already, we have upskilled over 1 million learners. We are looking forward to having you be a part of our family. 

 

We’ll see you at the finish line!
